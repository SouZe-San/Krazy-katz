ABOUT

KRAZY KATZ .

The world of motorcycle riding is male dominated and focused on men. But nearly 20% of motorcycle owners are women, not to mention the women who are passengers. These ladies need a one-stop-shop where they can purchase gear and clothing designed specifically for them at an affordable price. That's where I come in. My name is Katrina Whitney and I'm the owner of Krazy Katz Motor Gear, an all women's motorcycle gear and apparel shop.
